Biography

Prof. Pranawachandra Deshmukh (born 1957) is an Indian academic administrator and educationist. He currently serves as PM Professor at Central University of Kerala. He was the former Vice-Chancellor of Swami Ramanand Teerth Marathwada University (SRTMU), Nanded.

He earned his M.A. in Political Science and Ph.D. from Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Marathwada University. With over 42 years of teaching and administrative experience, he has been instrumental in implementing NEP 2020 reforms, establishing research centres, and promoting inclusive education. He has guided 28 doctoral students and published extensively on higher education governance, public policy, and quality assurance.

Prof. Deshmukh has served on numerous national committees, including UGC, NAAC, and AICTE, and has received multiple lifetime achievement awards for his contributions to Indian higher education.
