About

Prof. Pranawachandra Deshmukh is a renowned academic administrator and professor currently serving as PM Professor at Central University of Kerala. He previously held the position of Vice-Chancellor of Swami Ramanand Teerth Marathwada University (SRTMU), Nanded. With over three decades of experience in higher education, his work spans educational policy, governance, curriculum innovation, and promoting inclusive access to quality education. He has been instrumental in establishing multiple interdisciplinary centres and fostering international collaborations. Prof. Deshmukh continues to guide doctoral research and mentor young faculty members across Indian universities.
